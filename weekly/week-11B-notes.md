## I. More Canvas Core Skills

   - Docs:

     - Bezier Curves:
       - [`ctx.quadraticCurveTo()`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/CanvasRenderingContext2D/quadraticCurveTo)
